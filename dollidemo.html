What's Dolli?

This is my working demo for a girl-game idea that I have called Dolli. This will be a 2D mobile game centered around creative self-expression through fashion and character customization.

Concept Overview

View Prototype


Future Features

Dolli will start off as an avatar maker demo with options for skin tone, facial features, hair, clothing, and more.

  • Different body types/face shapes:
    Before I start adding more clothes, I’ll need to create another body type

  • Turn your Dolli into a sticker:
    There will be an option to order a sticker of the Dolli you create.
  • Create your own design:
    Use a template to create and send your own outfit ideas.

  • Dolli store:
    Buy outfits created by other users,or sell your own.

  • Minigames:
    Play minigames.
  • Personalized profiles:
    Add your interests, favorites, and your saved Dolli’s to your profile.
